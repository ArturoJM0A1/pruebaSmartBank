# Daily Scrum Template - SmartBank

## Daily Scrum Purpose

**Duration**: 15 minutes (maximum)
**Time**: Same time every day (e.g., 9:00 AM)
**Participants**: Development Team, Scrum Master
**Purpose**: Synchronize work, identify blockers

---

## Meeting Format

Each team member answers THREE questions:

1. **What did I accomplish yesterday?**
2. **What will I work on today?**
3. **Are there any blockers?**

## Common Mistakes

1. **Status report meeting**: Daily Scrum is for synchronization, not status updates
2. **Problem-solving**: Take detailed discussions offline
3. **Latecomers**: Start on time, end on time
4. **Missing team members**: Everyone should attend (except SM can skip occasionally)
5. **Too long**: If it exceeds 15 minutes, you're solving problems

---

## Best Practices

- Stand up (keeps meeting short)
- Same time, same place every day
- Focus on the three questions
- Park detailed discussions for after the meeting
- Update task board after the meeting
- Scrum Master removes blockers, not solves technical problems

---

## Blocker Resolution

| Blocker Type | Owner | Resolution |
|--------------|-------|------------|
| Technical | Developer | Pair programming, research |
| Dependency | Scrum Master | Coordinate with other teams |
| Resource | Product Owner | Re-prioritize or get resource |
| Knowledge | Team | Knowledge sharing session |
