# Sprint Review - SmartBank

## Sprint Review Purpose

**Duration**: 1-2 hours
**Participants**: Development Team, Product Owner, Stakeholders
**Purpose**: Demo completed work, gather feedback, adapt backlog

---

## Demo Checklist

### Before the Review
- [ ] All completed stories are deployed to staging
- [ ] Demo environment is working
- [ ] Presentation slides prepared (if needed)
- [ ] Stakeholders are invited

### During the Review
- [ ] Demo each completed story
- [ ] Show working software (not mockups)
- [ ] Explain technical decisions (briefly)
- [ ] Gather stakeholder feedback
- [ ] Discuss what was NOT completed and why

### After the Review
- [ ] Document feedback
- [ ] Update product backlog based on feedback
- [ ] Schedule follow-up items
- [ ] Celebrate team achievements

## Acceptance Criteria Verification

For each completed story, verify:
- [ ] All acceptance criteria met
- [ ] No critical bugs
- [ ] Works on mobile and desktop
- [ ] Performance is acceptable
- [ ] Accessibility standards met

---

## Sprint Review vs Sprint Demo

| Aspect | Sprint Review | Sprint Demo |
|--------|---------------|-------------|
| Purpose | Inspect and adapt | Show completed work |
| Audience | Team + Stakeholders | Stakeholders |
| Duration | 1-2 hours | 30-60 minutes |
| Focus | Process + Product | Product only |
